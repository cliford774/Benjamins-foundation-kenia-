Who We Are

Building Sustainable Pathways Out of Poverty

Benjamin’s Foundation Kenia emerged directly from a foundational commitment to address localized resource discrepancies facing vulnerable children and families. Based in Kenya, we approach community development through comprehensive structural transformations rather than short-term relief efforts.

By integrating global volunteer expertise alongside local initiative management, we maximize capital transparency and directly execute programs engineered for long-term self-sufficiency.
